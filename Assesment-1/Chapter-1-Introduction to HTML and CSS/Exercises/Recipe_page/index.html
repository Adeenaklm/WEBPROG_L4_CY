Chicken Biryani Recipe

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with tender chicken, fragrant spices, and herbs. Perfect for special occasions or a comforting meal.

Servings: 4-6

Preparation Time: 30 minutes (plus marinating time)

Cooking Time: 45-50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt, to taste

Procedure

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© onions in oil until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Cook tomatoes until soft, then add the marinated chicken along with spices. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  4. Prepare rice by boiling it with whole spices (cumin, cloves, cardamom, bay leaves, cinnamon) until half-cooked. Drain and set aside.
  5. Layer the rice over the chicken mixture, adding coriander and mint leaves between layers.
  6. Dum cook (steam) on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
