Overview

8 Ball Pool is an online multiplayer pool game developed by Miniclip. Players can compete in tournaments, play against friends, and win in-game coins to upgrade cues and tables.

Key Information

Developer: Miniclip

Release Date: 2010

Platforms: iOS, Android, Web

Genre: Sports, Arcade

Gameplay

8 Ball Pool follows traditional pool rules. Players take turns to pot either solids or stripes before attempting to pot the 8 ball to win the game.

Features

✔ Multiplayer mode

✔ Tournaments and leagues

✔ Customizable cues and tables

✔ Challenge friends online

Reviews

The game is praised for its smooth gameplay and competitive matchmaking but criticized for its in-game purchases.
