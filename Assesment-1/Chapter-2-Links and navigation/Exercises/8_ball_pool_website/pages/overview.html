Overview

8 Ball Pool is an online multiplayer game developed by Miniclip. The game is available on multiple platforms such as iOS, Android, and web browsers. It allows players to compete in matches with friends or random opponents around the world. The game features various modes, including 1v1 matches and tournaments, and it has become one of the most popular pool games worldwide.
